**Site:** mikejones.online (Ghost Pro)
**Fediverse Handle:** @mike@mikejones.online

## What is ActivityPub?

ActivityPub enables your Ghost site to be followed from Mastodon, Threads, Pixelfed, and other Fediverse platforms. When you publish a post, it appears in your followers' timelines automatically. Think of it as owning your own social media server that federates with 10+ million users.

---

## Quick Setup Steps

### Step 1: Access Ghost Admin (2 minutes)
1. Go to: https://mikejones.online/ghost
2. Log in with your credentials

### Step 2: Enable ActivityPub (5 minutes)
1. Click **Settings** in the left sidebar
2. Look for one of these sections:
   - **Membership** → Fediverse
   - **Growth** → Network
   - **Labs** → ActivityPub
   - **Beta Features** → Fediverse
3. Toggle the switch to **ON**
4. Wait for confirmation message (30-60 seconds)
5. Verify status shows "Connected" or "Active"

### Step 3: Configure Your Profile (10 minutes)
1. Set your **display name** (e.g., "Mike Jones")
2. Verify **username** is "mike" (creates @mike@mikejones.online)
3. Write your **bio** emphasizing AI/ML expertise:
   - Example: "AI/ML Engineer | Building intelligent systems | Portfolio at mikejones.online"
4. Upload **profile picture** (professional headshot, 400x400px minimum)
5. Upload **header image** (optional, reflects personal brand)
6. Enable **engagement display** (likes, boosts, replies)
7. Click **Save**

### Step 4: Test Federation (10 minutes)
1. **From Mastodon** (create account at mastodon.social if needed):
   - Search for: **@mike@mikejones.online**
   - Click **Follow**
   - Your Ghost profile should appear

2. **Publish Test Post** in Ghost:
   - Create new post: "Test - ActivityPub Federation"
   - Publish it (not draft)
   - Check Mastodon timeline - post should appear within 5 minutes

3. **Test Engagement:**
   - Like or boost the test post from Mastodon
   - Check Ghost admin notifications - interaction should appear

### Step 5: Launch Content (Ongoing)
1. Publish 2-3 initial posts (AI case studies) before promoting
2. Create introduction post explaining your focus
3. Use hashtags: #AI #MachineLearning #Python #Portfolio
4. Share your Fediverse handle on other platforms
5. Follow 10-20 AI/ML accounts on Mastodon
6. Engage daily with followers

## Troubleshooting

### "Not Properly Configured" Error
1. Disable the toggle
2. Clear browser cache (Cmd+Shift+Delete)
3. Close and reopen browser
4. Log back into Ghost admin
5. Re-enable the toggle
6. Wait 1-2 minutes

### Profile Not Searchable from Mastodon
1. Wait 10-15 minutes after activation (propagation delay)
2. Try searching from different Mastodon instances
3. Try: @mike@mikejones.online or mike@mikejones.online or https://mikejones.online
4. Verify ActivityPub shows "Active" in Ghost admin

### Posts Not Appearing in Timelines
1. Verify post is Published (not Draft)
2. Check if "Automatic federation" is enabled
3. Wait 5-10 minutes for federation
4. Verify followers are actually following
